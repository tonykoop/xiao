# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A single finished xiao — a Chinese end-blown vertical bamboo flute — standing upright as a long slender cylindrical tube roughly two feet tall, photographed as a clean studio product shot. The body is a smooth straight cylinder of warm dark-grained hardwood (black walnut, lightly figured, natural oil-and-wax finish) with a satin sheen, true cylindrical bore implied by the even wall and open foot end. The defining feature at the top is the end-blown embouchure: a crisp U-shaped notch cut into the rim of the mouthpiece end with a clean inner bevel, the way the player blows across rather than into a fipple. Down the front face march a vertical column of round tone holes (eight finger holes plus a thumb hole on the back), evenly spaced, dark open circles against the wood. The open foot end shows the round mouth of the bore. Neutral seamless light-gray studio background, soft directional studio light from upper left, gentle falloff, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, shallow but controlled depth of field, realistic scale cues, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

A xiao resting in a quiet woodworker's studio, propped vertically against a workbench of pale wood beside a coil of decorative wrapping thread and a small dish of finishing oil, soft natural window light raking from the side, motes of sawdust in the air. The dark walnut body glows warm against the muted workshop tones; the end-blown notch and the vertical line of tone holes catch the light. Environmental storytelling of a handmade instrument near completion, shallow depth of field with the flute sharp and the background softly blurred, calm contemplative mood, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the xiao's signature feature — the end-blown U-notch embouchure at the top of the mouthpiece. Fill the frame with the rim of the dark walnut tube: the clean U-shaped notch carved into the edge, the smooth inner bevel sloping into the bore, the open dark circle of the cylindrical bore beyond, and the satin grain of the hardwood. Raking low-angle light to emphasize the bevel geometry, the cut edge, and the wood figure. Extremely high detail, tactile material texture, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, fake maker marks, brand names, or carved characters; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no fipple/whistle mouthpiece, no recorder windway, no side-blown transverse hole, no keys or metal mechanism; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
