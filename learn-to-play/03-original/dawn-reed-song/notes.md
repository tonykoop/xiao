# Dawn Reed Song

**Composer:** Tony Koop / Heifer Zephyr (2025)
**License:** MIT — free to perform, record, arrange, and adapt
**Form:** Through-composed, 24 bars, ♩=52
**Instrument:** D xiao

## Story
The xiao's tone is associated with dawn and solitude in Chinese classical poetry.
This piece traces the movement of light across a reed bed at first light:
silence → first movement → full resonance → stillness again.

## Scale
D minor pentatonic: D, F, G, A, C. No raised 7th, no leading tone — the melody
resolves by falling, not rising.

## Practice tip
Play very slowly. The three sections (Emergence, Unfolding, Return) should feel like
three separate breaths. Long rests between them are intentional.
