# Hotaru Koi (蛍こい / Come Firefly)

**Tradition:** Trad Japanese children's song (pre-1900)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional folk melody
**Arrangement:** G major pentatonic for D xiao. Range G4–E5 (2nd octave).

## Practice tip
Slow and soft — this is a lullaby sung to fireflies at dusk.
The xiao's breathy lower register is perfect for this kind of intimate melody.
