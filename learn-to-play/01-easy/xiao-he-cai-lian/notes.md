# Xiao He Cai Lian (采莲 / Picking Lotus)

**Tradition:** Trad Chinese; associated with Tang Dynasty river poetry
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional folk melody
**Arrangement:** D pentatonic for D xiao (1st octave: D4–B4).

## Range check
Stays entirely in the first octave of the D xiao — suitable for a first session.

## Practice tip
A 6/8 feel: light and flowing, like oars dipping in still water.
No sharp accidentals — pure pentatonic. Rest in the silences between phrases.
