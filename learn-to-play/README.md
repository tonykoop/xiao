# learn-to-play / Xiao (D)

Starter songbook for the **D-tuned xiao** (洞箫) from the [`xiao`](https://github.com/tonykoop/xiao) build packet. Generated by the [`sheet-music`](https://github.com/tonykoop/sheet-music) skill.

The xiao is a vertical end-blown bamboo flute. The tone is breathy and deep; it favours slow, expressive playing.

Range: **D4 – D6** (two octaves). 6 finger holes + 1 thumb hole.

## Notes on xiao notation
Standard staff notation uses concert pitch. The xiao has a low, dark tone; play the written notes
one octave lower than a flute reading the same score (an octave transposing instrument in practice,
though not formally). ABC files here are written for the player's reading convenience.
