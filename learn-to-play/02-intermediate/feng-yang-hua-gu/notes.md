# Feng Yang Hua Gu (凤阳花鼓 / Flower Drum Song)

**Tradition:** Trad Chinese folk dance song, Ming Dynasty origin (pre-1650)
**Public domain:** Yes — traditional, pre-1929
**Arrangement:** G pentatonic for D xiao. Range G4–D5 (spans 1st–2nd octave boundary).

## Practice tip
Lively and dance-like — a contrast to the slower pieces in this collection.
The drum-song rhythm gives a strong sense of pulse: lock your breath to the beat.
