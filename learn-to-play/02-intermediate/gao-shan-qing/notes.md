# Gao Shan Qing (高山青 / High Mountain Trail)

**Tradition:** Trad Taiwanese / Hokkien folk; collected in pre-1929 sources
**Public domain:** Yes — pre-1929 (US)
**Arrangement:** D pentatonic across both octaves of the D xiao (D4–A5).

## Range check
Climbs from D4 (bottom of xiao) to A5 (upper register). The octave shift requires
opening the thumb hole mid-phrase. Practice the crossover point (D5 area) separately.

## Practice tip
This is an expansive, mountain-air tune. Give the upper octave notes extra breath —
the xiao's upper register needs slightly more pressure and firmer embouchure.
